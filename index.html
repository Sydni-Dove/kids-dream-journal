🌙 What Is a Dream or Vision?
😴

Dreams — while you SLEEP

While your body rests, God can show you things in your mind like a movie only you can see.

👁️

Visions — while you're AWAKE

Sometimes God shows pictures in your mind while you pray, worship, or think about Him.

Both can be messages from God! 🕊️

🔍 Dream Detective Steps
1

What did I see? 📝

Write everything you remember — even the weird parts!

2

Dream or vision? 💭

Did it happen while sleeping or while awake praying/worshipping?

3

Look for clues 🔎

Answer easy questions about animals, people, places, colors, feelings, and things that repeated.

4

Ask God 🙏

Pray first! Ask: "God, what were You saying to me?" Then write it down.

5

Apply it 🌱

How does it help your life? Write it out and share with a trusted adult!
